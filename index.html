Selected Projects

  • Eternity II - Convex Optimization Term Project
      Implementation of a solver for edge-matching puzzle problem, Eternity II. Based on J. Dattorro's modeling example ($4 , Convex Optimization and Euclidean Distances), we model and try to solve Eternity II (and identical subproblems) as a constrained convex optimization problem. We evaluate original problem's requirements in terms of memory space, and exploit sparsity in order to reduce its dimension. Due to CVX inaccuracy, we implement Simplex Algorithm (revised and not) in order to solve the required feasibility problems.
  • Switch Off the Lights - Analysis and Design of Telecom Modules Term Project
      Design and implementation of a custom low‐cost, multi-hop, wireless sensor network, that monitors whether lights are switched off in the monitored areas (i.e. classrooms) and more importantly, whether lights should or not be switched off (i.e. when people are inside). Human presence is monitored with low‐cost, clever ways, without privacy intrusion, and additional data is collected for further energy savings.
